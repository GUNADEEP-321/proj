Education

10th Class Student

Currently pursuing my 10th class in Andhra Pradesh, Chittoor, India.

Focusing on academics while exploring my passion for technology and coding.

I am currently a 10th class student in Andhra Pradesh, Chittoor district, India. Like most students my age, I am working hard to score well in my board exams and stay focused on academics. But alongside my studies, I have discovered something that excites me even more — technology and coding.

My interest in computers started with curiosity. I used to wonder how websites worked, how apps are made, and how games run so smoothly. This curiosity soon turned into a passion. I began exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and started building small websites and projects. Every time I create something, I feel proud — even if it’s just a simple webpage. It feels amazing to turn an idea into something real.

Despite being a student with a full academic schedule, I make time to learn coding through online resources, YouTube, and free platforms. I am especially interested in front-end web development, user interface design, and animations. One of my proudest achievements is building my own portfolio website, which I host online using Netlify.

Balancing school and coding isn’t always easy. I sometimes stay up late to finish a project or fix an error that I can’t stop thinking about. But every challenge teaches me something new, whether it’s debugging a script or solving a tough math problem.

In the future, I dream of becoming a software developer, and possibly launching my own startup. I want to keep learning, keep building, and one day, make tools or websites that help people.

I believe that age is not a limit when it comes to learning. With passion, dedication, and the right mindset, anything is possible — even for a 10th class student like me.
